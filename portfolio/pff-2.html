Collection on the Fly

Here's a bit more information as to what the problem was that we were trying to solve on this project

Project Details

Client: Pro Football Focus

Products: Data Collection

Framework: AngularJS, Phoenix

Highlights: Information design

Pro Football Focus then came back to us to craft a data collection application intended to replace the system of spreadsheets they kept on Microsoft Access database. With the provision of fine-grain game data being their bread and butter, there were quite the number of form fields to grapple with. I worked closely with company analysts to fine-tune the flow from datapoint to datapoint over the course of a play. When everything was said and done, analysts were wrapping up the collection of a given game's data within an hour of a game's completion, not days.
